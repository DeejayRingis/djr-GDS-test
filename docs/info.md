## How it works

Right now, this project just blinks a LED pattern on a seven segment display. The blinking can either be on a 1Hz clock, or on press of a step button. 

## How to test

Connect switch bar to inputs 0 through 7, and output LEDs to outputs 0 through 7. ON switch should correspond to an ON LED

## External hardware

LED Bar, 7 switch bar, 1Hz clock, pushbutton, Single Pole Double Throw Slide Switch
